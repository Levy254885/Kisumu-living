About Kisumu Living

Your trusted partner for real estate and vacation rentals in Kisumu

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Kisumu Living began as a small real estate agency with a passion for helping people find their perfect homes in Kisumu. Over the years, we've grown into a full-service property company offering real estate sales, rentals, and premium Airbnb accommodations.

Our team of local experts has in-depth knowledge of the Kisumu property market. We understand the unique needs of both residents and visitors to this beautiful lakeside city.

What sets us apart is our commitment to personalized service. Whether you're looking for a short-term stay or your forever home, we take the time to understand your needs and preferences.
